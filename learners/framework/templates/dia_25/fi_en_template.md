#### dia_25 — Disputing an unusually high electricity bill with power company
**Learner role:** B
**Non-learner gender:** female
**FI Konteksti:** Soitat sähköyhtiöön riitauttaaksesi poikkeuksellisen korkean sähkölaskun.
**EN Context:** You call the power company to dispute an unusually high electricity bill.

**FI Koko mallidialogi:**
- **A**: Sähkövirta Oy asiakaspalvelu, Hanna puhelimessa. Miten voin auttaa?
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: greet, introduce self, say you received your latest electricity bill and the amount is much higher than usual, ask for an explanation -->
- **A**: Selvä, mä katson sun tilille. Mikä sun asiakasnumero on?
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: give a customer number, say the bill is for January and it's over three hundred euros when it's normally around a hundred -->
- **A**: Okei, mä näen täällä tammikuun laskun. Kulutus oli kolmetuhattakaksisataa kilowattituntia. Se on kyllä selvästi enemmän kuin edellisinä kuukausina, jolloin kulutus oli noin tuhat kilowattituntia kuussa.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say that can't be right, nothing has changed at home — same appliances, same number of people, ask if the meter could be broken -->
- **A**: Se on mahdollista. Mä suosittelen, että tarkistat ensin mittarilukeman itse. Onko sulla etäluettava mittari vai vanha mittari?
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say you have a smart meter, ask how to check the readings yourself -->
- **A**: Hyvä, etäluettavasta mittarista mä näen tuntitason kulutuksen täältä järjestelmästä. Hetkinen. Joo, sun kulutus on ollut korkea erityisesti öisin, keskimäärin viisitoista kilowattituntia yössä. Se viittaa johonkin laitteeseen, joka on päällä koko yön.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say you don't know what could use that much power at night, ask if it could be the water heater or underfloor heating -->
- **A**: Lämminvesivaraaja tai lattialämmityksen termostaatti on yleisimmät syyt. Voi olla, että termostaatti on mennyt rikki ja lämmitys on päällä jatkuvasti. Suosittelen, että pyydät sähköasentajan tarkistamaan.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask if you can get a payment extension while this is being investigated, say you don't want to pay a bill that might be wrong -->
- **A**: Joo, mä voin siirtää laskun eräpäivää kahdella viikolla ilman lisämaksua. Jos kulutus osoittautuu mittarivirheeksi, korjataan lasku. Jos se on laitevika, niin valitettavasti kulutus on todellista.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask what to do next — should you file a formal complaint or is there a form to fill in -->
- **A**: Sä voit tehdä kirjallisen selvityspyynnön meille sähköpostilla osoitteeseen reklamaatiot ät sähkövirta piste fi tai nettisivujen lomakkeella. Laita viestiin asiakasnumero, mittarilukema ja kuvaus ongelmasta. Vastaamme neljäntoista päivän kuluessa. Mä merkitsen tähän, että laskun eräpäivää siirretään.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: thank Hanna for the help, confirm the payment extension, say you'll send the complaint today, say goodbye -->
- **A**: Ole hyvä, {NIMI}. Toivottavasti asia selviää pian. Soita uudestaan, jos tulee kysymyksiä. Hei hei!

**EN Full sample dialogue:**
- **A**: Sähkövirta Oy customer service, Hanna speaking. How can I help?
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: greet, introduce self, say you received your latest electricity bill and the amount is much higher than usual, ask for an explanation -->
- **A**: Alright, let me check your account. What's your customer number?
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: give a customer number, say the bill is for January and it's over three hundred euros when it's normally around a hundred -->
- **A**: Okay, I can see the January bill here. The consumption was 3,200 kilowatt-hours. That's clearly more than previous months, when consumption was about 1,000 kilowatt-hours per month.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say that can't be right, nothing has changed at home — same appliances, same number of people, ask if the meter could be broken -->
- **A**: That's possible. I recommend you first check the meter reading yourself. Do you have a smart meter or an old meter?
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say you have a smart meter, ask how to check the readings yourself -->
- **A**: Good, from the smart meter I can see hourly consumption data from our system. One moment. Yes, your consumption has been high especially at night, averaging 15 kilowatt-hours per night. That suggests some device running all night.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say you don't know what could use that much power at night, ask if it could be the water heater or underfloor heating -->
- **A**: A hot water tank or underfloor heating thermostat are the most common causes. It's possible the thermostat has broken and the heating is running constantly. I recommend having an electrician check it.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask if you can get a payment extension while this is being investigated, say you don't want to pay a bill that might be wrong -->
- **A**: Yes, I can extend the bill's due date by 2 weeks at no extra charge. If the consumption turns out to be a meter error, we'll correct the bill. If it's a device fault, then unfortunately the consumption is real.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask what to do next — should you file a formal complaint or is there a form to fill in -->
- **A**: You can submit a written inquiry to us by email at reklamaatiot at sähkövirta dot fi, or through the form on our website. Include your customer number, meter reading, and description of the problem. We'll respond within 14 days. I'll note here that the bill's due date is being extended.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: thank Hanna for the help, confirm the payment extension, say you'll send the complaint today, say goodbye -->
- **A**: You're welcome, {NAME}. I hope the matter gets resolved soon. Call again if you have questions. Bye bye!
