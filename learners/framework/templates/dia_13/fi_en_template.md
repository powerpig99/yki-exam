#### dia_13 — Discussing teenager's school progress report with teacher
**Learner role:** B
**Non-learner gender:** female
**FI Konteksti:** Tapaat lapsesi opettajan ja keskustelette teini-ikäisen todistuksesta ja kouluarvosanoista.
**EN Context:** You meet your child's teacher and discuss the teenager's school report card and grades.

**FI Koko mallidialogi:**
- **A**: Hei {NIMI}, kiva kun pääsit tulemaan. Mä olen Tiina, Samulin luokanvalvoja. Istutaanko alas ja käydään todistus läpi?
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: greet, say glad to be here, ask how Samuli has been doing this semester overall -->
- **A**: Samuli on yleisesti ottaen pärjännyt hyvin. Historian ja biologian arvosanat nousi kahdeksasta yhdeksään. Hän on selvästi kiinnostunut niistä aineista.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: express happiness about that, ask about math — child mentioned it has been difficult -->
- **A**: Matikka on kyllä tippunut seiskasta kuutoseen. Mä olen huomannut, että Samuli ei aina palauta kotitehtäviä ajallaan. Se vaikuttaa arvosanaan.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say did not know about missed homework, ask if there is extra support available at school -->
- **A**: Meidän koulussa on tukiopetusta keskiviikkoisin kello neljästä viiteen. Se on ilmaista ja siellä on matikan opettaja auttamassa. Mä suosittelen sitä Samulille.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say will make sure child attends, ask about Finnish language grade — it seems to have stayed the same -->
- **A**: Äidinkielessä Samuli on seiskalla. Hän lukee hyvin ja ymmärtää tekstejä, mutta kirjalliset tuotokset on liian lyhyitä. Kirjoittamisessa pitää harjoitella enemmän.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask what kind of writing practice would help at home -->
- **A**: Lukupäiväkirjan kirjoittaminen auttaa paljon. Jos Samuli lukisi yhen kirjan kuussa ja kirjoittaisi siitä lyhyen arvion, se kehittäisi kirjoitustaitoa. Mä voin ehdottaa sopivia kirjoja.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say that is a good idea, ask about behavior and how child gets along with classmates -->
- **A**: Samuli on mukava ja tulee kaikkien kanssa hyvin toimeen. Välitunneilla hän on usein kavereiden kanssa pelaamassa koripalloa. Mitään ongelmia ei ole ollut.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say relieved to hear that, ask about the upcoming parent-teacher evening and when it is -->
- **A**: Seuraava vanhempainilta on kolmannentenatoista maaliskuuta kello kuudelta. Siellä puhutaan yhdeksännen luokan kurssivalinnoista ja lukioon tai ammattikouluun hakemisesta.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say will definitely attend, thank Tiina for thorough overview, ask if can contact her through Wilma -->
- **A**: Totta kai. Laita Wilmaan viestiä milloin vain. Mä vastaan yleensä saman päivän aikana. Kiitos kun tulit ja pidetään yhteyttä. Terveisiä Samulille!

**EN Full sample dialogue:**
- **A**: Hi {NAME}, nice that you could make it. I'm Tiina, Samuli's homeroom teacher. Shall we sit down and go through the report card?
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: greet, say glad to be here, ask how Samuli has been doing this semester overall -->
- **A**: Overall Samuli has been doing well. His history and biology grades went up from eight to nine. He's clearly interested in those subjects.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: express happiness about that, ask about math — child mentioned it has been difficult -->
- **A**: Math has indeed dropped from seven to six. I've noticed that Samuli doesn't always submit homework on time. That affects the grade.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say did not know about missed homework, ask if there is extra support available at school -->
- **A**: Our school has remedial teaching on Wednesdays from four to five. It's free and a math teacher is there to help. I recommend it for Samuli.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say will make sure child attends, ask about Finnish language grade — it seems to have stayed the same -->
- **A**: In Finnish, Samuli is at a seven. He reads well and understands texts, but his written work is too short. He needs more writing practice.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask what kind of writing practice would help at home -->
- **A**: Writing a reading diary helps a lot. If Samuli read one book a month and wrote a short review of it, that would develop his writing skills. I can suggest suitable books.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say that is a good idea, ask about behavior and how child gets along with classmates -->
- **A**: Samuli is friendly and gets along well with everyone. During breaks he's often playing basketball with friends. There haven't been any problems.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say relieved to hear that, ask about the upcoming parent-teacher evening and when it is -->
- **A**: The next parent-teacher evening is on March 13th at six o'clock. It will cover ninth-grade course selections and applying to upper secondary or vocational school.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: say will definitely attend, thank Tiina for thorough overview, ask if can contact her through Wilma -->
- **A**: Of course. Send a message on Wilma anytime. I usually reply within the same day. Thank you for coming and let's stay in touch. Say hi to Samuli!
