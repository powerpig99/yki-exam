#### dia_32 — Calling taxi service for early morning airport transfer
**Learner role:** A
**Non-learner gender:** male
**FI Konteksti:** Soitat taksipalveluun ja varaat taksin aikaiseen aamukuljetukseen lentoasemalle.
**EN Context:** You call a taxi service to book an early morning airport transfer.

**FI Koko mallidialogi:**
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: greet, say you need a taxi for early morning next Tuesday to Helsinki-Vantaa airport, your flight leaves at seven fifteen -->
- **B**: Hei, Taksi Helsinki, Antti puhelimessa. Selvä, mihin aikaan sun pitää olla kentällä ja mistä osoitteesta haetaan?
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say you should be at the airport by five thirty at the latest, give your home address -->
- **B**: Okei, siihen aikaan matka kestää noin kolmekymmentä neljäkymmentä minuuttia, koska aamulla ei oo vielä ruuhkaa. Mä laitan noudon kello neljä viisikymmentä, niin ollaan hyvin ajoissa.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask how much the trip costs and if the price is fixed or metered -->
- **B**: Lentoasemakuljetus on kiinteähintainen. Hinta sun osoitteesta kentälle on neljäkymmentäyhdeksän euroa. Siihen sisältyy matkatavaroiden lastaus. Yöaikaan kello kuuden ja yhdeksän välillä ei oo lisämaksua, koska meillä on kiinteä hinta.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask if they accept card payment or if you need cash -->
- **B**: Kortilla maksu onnistuu ihan hyvin autossa. Käy myös MobilePay ja käteinen. Voit myös maksaa etukäteen netissä, niin homma on hoidossa.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say card payment is fine, ask if you can get a bigger car because you have two large suitcases and a carry-on -->
- **B**: Kyllä, mä laitan tilaukseen farmariauton tai tila-auton. Kaksi isoa matkalaukkua ja käsimatkatavarat mahtuu hyvin. Sille ei tuu lisähintaa.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask how you'll recognize the taxi — will they call or send a message when they arrive -->
- **B**: Kuljettaja soittaa sulle, kun on ovella. Saat myös tekstiviestin viisi minuuttia ennen, jossa on auton rekisterinumero ja kuljettajan nimi. Taksi odottaa korkeintaan viisi minuuttia ilmaiseksi.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask what happens if your flight is cancelled or you need to cancel the taxi -->
- **B**: Peruutus onnistuu ilmaiseksi kuusi tuntia ennen noutoaikaa. Sen jälkeen peritään kymmenen euron peruutusmaksu. Jos lento myöhästyy, soita meille niin siirretään aikaa.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say you'd also need a return taxi from the airport on Friday evening, ask if you can book that now too -->
- **B**: Totta kai. Palatessa hinta on sama, neljäkymmentäyhdeksän euroa. Laita meille vaan tekstiviesti, kun oot laskeutunut, niin me lähetetään auto. Yleensä odotusaika kentällä on kymmenen viisitoista minuuttia.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: confirm both bookings — Tuesday pickup at 4:50 AM and Friday return from airport, thank Antti -->
- **B**: Hienoa, molemmat on varattu. Saat vahvistuksen tekstiviestillä. Hyvää matkaa, {NIMI}, ja hei hei!

**EN Full sample dialogue:**
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: greet, say you need a taxi for early morning next Tuesday to Helsinki-Vantaa airport, your flight leaves at seven fifteen -->
- **B**: Hi, Taksi Helsinki, Antti speaking. Alright, what time do you need to be at the airport and what address should we pick you up from?
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say you should be at the airport by five thirty at the latest, give your home address -->
- **B**: Okay, at that time the trip takes about 30 to 40 minutes since there's no traffic yet in the morning. I'll set the pickup for 4:50 AM, so you'll be there in plenty of time.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask how much the trip costs and if the price is fixed or metered -->
- **B**: The airport transfer has a fixed price. From your address to the airport it's 49 euros. That includes luggage loading. There's no night surcharge between 6 AM and 9 PM since we have a fixed price.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask if they accept card payment or if you need cash -->
- **B**: Card payment works perfectly in the car. We also accept MobilePay and cash. You can also pay in advance online, so everything is sorted.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say card payment is fine, ask if you can get a bigger car because you have two large suitcases and a carry-on -->
- **B**: Yes, I'll put in an estate car or a van for the order. Two large suitcases and carry-on luggage will fit easily. There's no extra charge for that.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask how you'll recognize the taxi — will they call or send a message when they arrive -->
- **B**: The driver will call you when they're at the door. You'll also get a text message 5 minutes before with the car's license plate number and driver's name. The taxi waits up to 5 minutes for free.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask what happens if your flight is cancelled or you need to cancel the taxi -->
- **B**: Cancellation is free up to 6 hours before pickup time. After that there's a 10-euro cancellation fee. If the flight is delayed, call us and we'll adjust the time.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say you'd also need a return taxi from the airport on Friday evening, ask if you can book that now too -->
- **B**: Of course. The return trip is the same price, 49 euros. Just text us when you've landed and we'll send a car. Typically the wait time at the airport is 10 to 15 minutes.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: confirm both bookings — Tuesday pickup at 4:50 AM and Friday return from airport, thank Antti -->
- **B**: Great, both are booked. You'll receive a confirmation by text. Have a good trip, {NAME}, and bye bye!
