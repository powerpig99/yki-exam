#### dia_04 — Opening a savings account at bank
**Learner role:** A
**Non-learner gender:** female
**FI Konteksti:** Käyt pankissa avaamassa säästötiliä ja kysyt eri vaihtoehdoista, koroista ja ehdoista.
**EN Context:** You visit a bank to open a savings account and ask about different options, interest rates, and terms.

**FI Koko mallidialogi:**
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: greet the bank clerk, say you want to open a savings account, ask what options are available -->
- **B**: Hei, tervetuloa! Meillä on tavallinen säästötili ja määräaikaistalletus. Tavallisella säästötilillä korko on kaksi pilkku viisi prosenttia vuodessa.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask about the fixed-term deposit — how does it differ and what is the interest -->
- **B**: Määräaikaistalletuksessa sidotte rahat esimerkiksi kahdellatoista kuukaudella. Korko on parempi, kolme pilkku kaksi prosenttia, mutta rahoja ei voi nostaa kesken kauden ilman kertyneiden korkojen menetystä.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask about the minimum deposit amounts for both options -->
- **B**: Tavalliseen säästötiliin ei ole minimiä. Määräaikaistalletuksen minimi on tuhat euroa. Molemmissa lähdevero on kolmekymmentä prosenttia ja se pidätetään automaattisesti.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say you want to start with the regular savings account, ask what documents are needed -->
- **B**: Tarvitsen passin tai henkilökortin. Ajokortti ei valitettavasti käy. Tilin avaus hoituu heti tässä ja tili näkyy verkkopankissa heti.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: hand over passport, ask if you can set up automatic monthly transfers to the savings account -->
- **B**: Kyllä, voitte tehdä toistuvaissiirron verkkopankissa. Valitsette summan ja päivämäärän niin raha siirtyy automaattisesti joka kuukausi.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask if there are any monthly fees for the savings account -->
- **B**: Ei, säästötili on täysin maksuton. Ainoat kulut ovat lähdevero koroista. Haluatteko avata tilin nyt?
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say yes, want to transfer 500 euros right away, thank Päivi -->
- **B**: Hienoa, mä teen siirron heti. Tervetuloa uudelleen milloin vaan! Hyvää päivän jatkoa!

**EN Full sample dialogue:**
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: greet the bank clerk, say you want to open a savings account, ask what options are available -->
- **B**: Hi, welcome! We have a regular savings account and a fixed-term deposit. The regular savings account has an interest rate of 2.5 percent per year.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask about the fixed-term deposit — how does it differ and what is the interest -->
- **B**: With a fixed-term deposit, you lock the money for example for 12 months. The interest is better, 3.2 percent, but you can't withdraw the money mid-term without losing the accrued interest.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask about the minimum deposit amounts for both options -->
- **B**: There's no minimum for the regular savings account. The minimum for a fixed-term deposit is 1000 euros. In both cases, the withholding tax is 30 percent and it's deducted automatically.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say you want to start with the regular savings account, ask what documents are needed -->
- **B**: I need a passport or ID card. A driving license unfortunately won't do. The account can be opened right here and it will appear in online banking immediately.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: hand over passport, ask if you can set up automatic monthly transfers to the savings account -->
- **B**: Yes, you can set up a recurring transfer in online banking. You choose the amount and date and the money transfers automatically every month.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask if there are any monthly fees for the savings account -->
- **B**: No, the savings account is completely free. The only costs are withholding tax on interest. Would you like to open the account now?
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say yes, want to transfer 500 euros right away, thank Päivi -->
- **B**: Great, I'll make the transfer right now. Welcome back anytime! Have a good day!
