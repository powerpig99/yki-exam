#### dia_28 — Calling occupational health about stress and sleep problems
**Learner role:** A
**Non-learner gender:** male
**FI Konteksti:** Soitat työterveyteen stressin ja unihäiriöiden takia ja varaat ajan.
**EN Context:** You call occupational health about stress and sleep problems and book an appointment.

**FI Koko mallidialogi:**
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: greet, say you'd like to book an appointment, you've been having trouble sleeping and feeling stressed for several weeks -->
- **B**: Hei, työterveys Terveystalo, Kari puhelimessa. Totta kai, kerrotko vähän tarkemmin, millaisia oireita sulla on?
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: explain that you wake up at three or four in the morning and can't fall back asleep, during the day you feel exhausted and can't concentrate at work -->
- **B**: Okei, kuulostaa ikävältä. Onko sulla muita oireita, esimerkiksi päänsärkyä, sydämentykytystä tai ahdistuksen tunnetta?
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say yes, you get headaches almost daily and sometimes feel anxious, especially on Sunday evenings before the work week -->
- **B**: Selvä. Onko töissä tapahtunut jotain muutoksia viime aikoina, esimerkiksi lisääntynyt työmäärä tai uusi tehtävä?
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say your team has been short-staffed for two months and you've been doing overtime almost every week, you feel overwhelmed -->
- **B**: Ymmärrän. Tää kuulostaa työuupumuksen oireilta. Mä suosittelen, että tulet vastaanotolle, jotta me voidaan arvioida sun tilanne kokonaisvaltaisesti.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: agree, ask about available times — preferably this week -->
- **B**: Meillä on aika huomenna iltapäivällä kello neljätoista kolmekymmentä tai torstaina aamulla kello yhdeksän. Vastaanotto kestää noin neljäkymmentäviisi minuuttia, koska käydään läpi myös kyselylomake.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: take the appointment for tomorrow at 2:30 PM, ask if you should prepare anything beforehand -->
- **B**: Täytä etukäteen uupumuskyselylomake, jonka mä lähetän sulle sähköpostilla. Se auttaa lääkäriä arvioimaan tilanteen nopeammin. Jos sulla on työnantajan sairausvakuutustiedot, ota nekin mukaan.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask if the visit is free through occupational health or if there's a fee -->
- **B**: Työterveyskäynti on sulle ilmainen. Työnantaja maksaa sen. Jos lääkäri kirjoittaa sairauslomaa, niin sekin hoituu meiltä suoraan työnantajalle.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask how long sick leave is typically given for stress-related issues -->
- **B**: Se riippuu tilanteesta. Yleensä alussa yhdestä kahteen viikkoa, ja sen jälkeen arvioidaan uudestaan. Tärkeintä on, että saadaan uni kuntoon ja stressin syyt käsiteltyä. Me voidaan tarvittaessa ohjata sut myös psykologille.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say that sounds good, confirm the appointment for tomorrow at 2:30, thank Kari -->
- **B**: Hyvä, mä varaan ajan sulle. Muista täyttää se lomake etukäteen. Pidä itsestäsi huolta, {NIMI}, ja nähdään huomenna. Hei hei!

**EN Full sample dialogue:**
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: greet, say you'd like to book an appointment, you've been having trouble sleeping and feeling stressed for several weeks -->
- **B**: Hi, occupational health Terveystalo, Kari speaking. Of course, can you tell me a bit more about what symptoms you're having?
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: explain that you wake up at three or four in the morning and can't fall back asleep, during the day you feel exhausted and can't concentrate at work -->
- **B**: Okay, that sounds unpleasant. Do you have other symptoms, for example headaches, heart palpitations, or feelings of anxiety?
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say yes, you get headaches almost daily and sometimes feel anxious, especially on Sunday evenings before the work week -->
- **B**: I see. Have there been any changes at work recently, for example increased workload or a new role?
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say your team has been short-staffed for two months and you've been doing overtime almost every week, you feel overwhelmed -->
- **B**: I understand. This sounds like burnout symptoms. I recommend you come in for an appointment so we can assess your situation comprehensively.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: agree, ask about available times — preferably this week -->
- **B**: We have a slot tomorrow afternoon at 2:30 PM or Thursday morning at 9 AM. The appointment takes about 45 minutes because we'll also go through a questionnaire.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: take the appointment for tomorrow at 2:30 PM, ask if you should prepare anything beforehand -->
- **B**: Fill in the burnout questionnaire beforehand, which I'll send you by email. It helps the doctor assess the situation faster. If you have your employer's health insurance details, bring those too.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask if the visit is free through occupational health or if there's a fee -->
- **B**: The occupational health visit is free for you. Your employer pays for it. If the doctor writes a sick leave, that's also handled from our end directly to the employer.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: ask how long sick leave is typically given for stress-related issues -->
- **B**: It depends on the situation. Usually 1 to 2 weeks initially, and then reassessed. The most important thing is to get your sleep sorted out and address the causes of stress. If needed, we can also refer you to a psychologist.
- **A**: <!-- LEARNER: say that sounds good, confirm the appointment for tomorrow at 2:30, thank Kari -->
- **B**: Good, I'll book the appointment for you. Remember to fill in the form beforehand. Take care, {NAME}, and see you tomorrow. Bye bye!
