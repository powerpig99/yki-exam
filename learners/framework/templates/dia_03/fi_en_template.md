#### dia_03 — Asking neighbor about building sauna schedule and laundry room rules
**Learner role:** B
**Non-learner gender:** female
**FI Konteksti:** Kysyt naapurilta taloyhtiön saunavuoroista ja pesutuvan säännöistä.
**EN Context:** You ask your neighbor about the building's sauna schedule and laundry room rules.

**FI Koko mallidialogi:**
- **A**: Moi {NIMI}! Mä näin et sä olit tulossa pyykkien kanssa. Onko kaikki menny hyvin uudessa asunnossa?
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: greet Tiina, say you just moved in last week, ask how the laundry room booking works -->
- **A**: Pesutupa varataan tuosta ilmoitustaulun vieressä olevasta varauskirjasta. Kirjoitat nimen ja kellonajan. Vuoroja on aamusta iltaan, kolme tuntia kerrallaan.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask what times are available and if there are specific days for each apartment -->
- **A**: Vuorot on maanantaista lauantaihin kello seitsemästä kello kymmeneen illalla. Ei oo määrättyjä päiviä, vaan se menee kuka ehtii ensin. Sunnuntaina pesutupa on kiinni.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask about the rules — can you leave laundry in the drying room overnight -->
- **A**: Kuivaushuoneessa voi pitää pyykit yön yli mutta ne pitää hakea seuraavana aamuna. Jos jättää pidemmäksi aikaa, tulee varoitus hallitukselta. Mä opin sen kantapään kautta.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: laugh, ask about the sauna — how do you book a sauna turn -->
- **A**: Saunavuorot varataan samasta kirjasta. Taloyhtiössä on kaksi saunavuoroa viikossa, torstaina ja lauantaina. Torstai on kello kuudestatoista kahteenkymmeneenyhteen ja lauantai kello neljästätoista kahteenkymmeneenyhteen.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask if there is a separate sauna fee or if it is included in the maintenance charge -->
- **A**: Se sisältyy vastikkeeseen, ei maksa mitään ekstraa. Mutta pitää muistaa pestä lauteet jälkeenpäin ja sammuttaa kiuas viimeisen jälkeen. Siivousaineet on kaapissa.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask how many people can use the sauna at the same time, and if you can bring a friend -->
- **A**: Sauna on aika pieni, neljä ihmistä mahtuu. Vieraat on sallittuja mutta sun pitää olla itse paikalla. Isännöitsijä ei tykkää jos ulkopuoliset käyttää saunaa ilman asukasta.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: thank Tiina for the help, say it is nice to have a friendly neighbor -->
- **A**: Eipä kestä! Jos tulee muita kysymyksiä, kopauta vaan mun oveen. Mä asun tossa ykkösen A:ssa. Tervetuloa taloon!

**EN Full sample dialogue:**
- **A**: Hi {NAME}! I saw you coming with laundry. Has everything gone well in the new apartment?
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: greet Tiina, say you just moved in last week, ask how the laundry room booking works -->
- **A**: The laundry room is booked from the reservation book next to the notice board. You write your name and time slot. Slots run from morning to evening, three hours each.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask what times are available and if there are specific days for each apartment -->
- **A**: Slots are Monday to Saturday from 7 AM to 10 PM. There are no assigned days, it's first come first served. The laundry room is closed on Sundays.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask about the rules — can you leave laundry in the drying room overnight -->
- **A**: You can leave laundry in the drying room overnight but you have to pick it up the next morning. If you leave it longer, you get a warning from the board. I learned that the hard way.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: laugh, ask about the sauna — how do you book a sauna turn -->
- **A**: Sauna turns are booked from the same book. The building has two sauna slots per week, Thursday and Saturday. Thursday is from 4 PM to 9 PM and Saturday from 2 PM to 9 PM.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask if there is a separate sauna fee or if it is included in the maintenance charge -->
- **A**: It's included in the maintenance charge, no extra cost. But you have to remember to wash the benches afterwards and turn off the stove after the last person. Cleaning supplies are in the cabinet.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: ask how many people can use the sauna at the same time, and if you can bring a friend -->
- **A**: The sauna is quite small, four people fit. Guests are allowed but you have to be present yourself. The property manager doesn't like it if outsiders use the sauna without a resident.
- **B**: <!-- LEARNER: thank Tiina for the help, say it is nice to have a friendly neighbor -->
- **A**: No problem! If you have other questions, just knock on my door. I live in apartment 1 A right there. Welcome to the building!
